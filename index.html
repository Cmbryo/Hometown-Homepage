The Old Mill

The Mill in Upper Slaughter dates back to 1658. A mill has existed in the village since 1086, as recorded in The Domesday Book.

St Edward's Church

Local legend says that J. R. R. Tolkien visited this church and the entrance inspired his Doors of Durin in The Lord of the Rings trilogy.

The Manor House

There's been a manor house in the village since 1004 AD, which has been completely renovated into a luxurious five-star hotel.
